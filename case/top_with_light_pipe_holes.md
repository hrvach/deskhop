# Explanation of top_with_light_pipe_hole.stl

## Purpose 
This 3D model as two 2mm holes added to allow placement of acrylic rods that transmit the light from the LEDs to be visible from outside the case.

## Modification Process
Imported the existing top.stl file into TinkerCAD. Measured location of status LEDs on the board as preassembled from Elecrow. Created 2mm "hole" cylinders and moved them into place. Union-grouped the previous top model with the "hole" cylinders and exported the altered model as an .stl file.

## Instructions for Use
After printing the model with PLA filament the holes were slightly too small for the 2mm acrylic rods I had obtained. I needed to drill out the holes very slightly to get a snug fit. After some experimentation adjusting it in this way provided the best fit for me. Cut 10mm sections of the acrylic rod with a utility knife and press into the holes. If the hole becomes too large the rod can be held in place with hot glue. I was able to obtain the acrylic rod I needed from [Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B08LCMS68J?ref=ppx_yo2ov_dt_b_fed_asin_title&th=1) This is exceedingly more acrylic rod that is needed for the case.
